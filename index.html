About GTA 6

Grand Theft Auto VI (GTA 6) is the upcoming installment in Rockstar Games' legendary open-world franchise. Set in the fictional state of Leonida (inspired by Florida), the game will feature a dual protagonist system with Jason and Lucia.

Release Date: May 26, 2026
Platforms: PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S (PC TBD)
Developer: Rockstar Games

The game promises an expansive open world, next-gen graphics, and an evolving story driven by player choices. More trailers and updates are expected leading up to launch.
